\chapter*{Abstract}

%motivation
Many merchants on e-commerce platforms utilize dynamic pricing to quickly adapt to new market situations and increase their profits. %generalization: many merchants?
The optimal pricing policy depends not only on external factors like competitors' pricing strategies but also on internal factors like inventory levels. %better sentence
%problem statement
The problem is finding a pricing strategy and inventory strategy that go together in order to maximize the profit when the demand is unknown.
